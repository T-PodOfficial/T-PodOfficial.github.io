About

T-Pod is open-source project. T-Pod is something that can maintain temperature of your drink. It's powered by Raspberry Pi 4B 4GB RAM and NodeMCU. Here's one Peltier's element and two resistance wires for maintaning temperature.

As the name indicates, T-Pod was primary invented only for maintaining tea temperature, but as time went, we rethink it and it ends were we are now.

Because we're not selling T-Pod, you can build it at home. You can find tutorial here
